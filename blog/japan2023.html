The short trip to Japan in 2023 January.

In early 2023, an unexpected journey unfolded as plans were set in motion to explore the landscapes of Japan. Packed in two days and Embarked from YYZ with a brief stopover at DTW, the flight proved to be remarkably seamless. The entire expedition unfolded as a captivating odyssey, with visits to renowned attractions and serene temples adding a layer of fascination to the experience.

Tokyo

Kichijoji - Inokashira Park

Shinjuku - Kabukicho

Taito - Senso-ji

Minato - Shiokazi Park

Fujisawa & Kamakura

Enoshima stands as an ideal destination for a brief day trip just beyond the bustling city of Tokyo. This petite island is easily accessible with a short train journey from Shinjuku. On a day blessed with clear skies, the enchanting sight of Mount Fuji graces the horizon, mirroring its majesty in the tranquil bay waters. As the sun gracefully sets, casting a warm glow upon the quaint seaside town, Enoshima becomes a picturesque haven for those seeking a delightful escape from the urban hustle and bustle.

Kyoto

Kyoto emerges as a captivating destination in Japan, renowned for its iconic temples and the majestic Fushimi Inari Taisha. Nestled amidst the gentle slopes of the mountains, these cultural treasures beckon visitors to embark on a brief yet rewarding hike to fully immerse themselves in their timeless charm.
