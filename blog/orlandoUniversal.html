Universal Studios

In contrast, Universal Studios prioritizes the immersive experience within each distinct theme throughout the theme park. Boasting a trio of roller coasters, including two captivating indoor coasters, the park further elevates the thrills with an array of 3D/4D experiences.

• Minion Blast - ★ ★ ☆ ☆ ☆

Islands of Advanture

Islands of Adventure in Orlando invites visitors to embark on an extraordinary journey through its diverse and thrilling themed islands. From the spellbinding wonders of The Wizarding World of Harry Potter to the prehistoric adventures of Jurassic Park, the park offers a captivating blend of immersive experiences and exhilarating attractions for guests of all ages.

• Harry Potter and the Forbidden Journey - ★ ★ ★ ★ ★
